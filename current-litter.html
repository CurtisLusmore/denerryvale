Current Litter

When we are planning to have another litter, we will advise of the anticipated date of arrival on our Dogzonline and Dogs of NSW advertisements. Once safely delivered, we will provide details of the litter in both our Dogzonline advertisement and our Denerry Vale Facebook page.

All pups will come with Dogs NSW Registration, will be microchipped, wormed, vaccinated, vet checked and will come with a puppy pack of information and accessories to help your puppy settle into its new home.

If you are interested in contacting us about one of our puppies, could you please (in the first instance) send an email to Denise Reynolds at denerryvale@hotmail.com or send a message to our Denerry Vale Facebook page.

We are very eager to place our puppies with only the best of homes and would appreciate your enquiry including some information to help us ascertain this, including:

  • your name,
  • location,
  • family members,
  • size and security of yard,
  • other pets,
  • familiarity with the breed, and
  • plans for providing a suitable lifestyle for your border collie.

Please feel free to ask any questions you may have, that we may have overlooked in providing on our website.
